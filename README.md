# 📊 Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimization Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing demand patterns and building a supply chain intelligence dashboard using Excel and Power BI.

The goal is to understand:
- How demand behaves over time
- What factors influence demand (seasonality, promotions, weekends)
- How to support logistics and supply decisions

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement
In FMCG and retail industries, inefficient demand planning can lead to:
- Stockouts (lost sales)
- Overstocking (increased costs)

This project aims to provide data-driven insights for better demand planning and inventory decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering)
- Microsoft Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Store Item Demand Forecasting Dataset (Kaggle)
- Includes:
  - Date-wise sales
  - Store and item level data
  - Price and promotion information

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis

### 📌 1. Demand & Pattern Analysis
- Demand trends over time
- Monthly seasonality
- Weekend vs weekday behavior
- Promotion impact on demand
- Top demand-driving products

---

### 📌 2. Supply Chain & Logistics Insights
- Store-wise demand prioritization
- Product-wise demand distribution
- Store × Item demand mapping (Matrix)
- Identification of high-demand products
- Demand variability analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔷 Page 1: Demand & Patterns
- Total Sales, Avg Demand, Peak Demand
- Time-series demand trend
- Seasonal patterns
- Promo & weekend impact
- Top products (Pareto analysis)

---

### 🔷 Page 2: Logistics & Supply Intelligence
- Supply priority by store
- Top demand drivers
- Store-wise demand distribution
- Demand variability for selected items
- Interactive slicers (store, item, promo)

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Demand shows strong seasonal patterns
- Promotions significantly increase demand
- Few products contribute majority of sales
- Demand varies across stores → requires location-based supply
- Demand variability indicates need for flexible inventory planning

---

## 🎯 Business Impact
- Helps reduce stockouts
- Improves inventory planning
- Supports data-driven supply chain decisions
